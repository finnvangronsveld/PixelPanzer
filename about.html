About PixelPanzer

PixelPanzer is a retro-inspired tank shooter game developed by four students—Wesley SW., Andreas, Wesley B., and Finn —from Thomas More University. Our goal was to create an engaging and accessible gaming experience that combines classic gameplay with modern innovations.

One of the key features of PixelPanzer is its integration with an accessible controller using ultrasound sensors. This allows players with limited mobility to control their tank using hand movements or other non-traditional inputs, making the game more inclusive and adaptable for different users.

Through this project, we explored game development, hardware integration, and user accessibility, aiming to push the boundaries of interactive gaming. We hope PixelPanzer provides an exciting and fun experience for everyone!
